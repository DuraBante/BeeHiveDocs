Create a Procedure

How to create a procedure

Video Guide

Walk Through

a) Click on “Stored Procedures” in the Manager section of the left menu.

b) If this is the very first Procedure being created for the hive, skip to Step 7c. Otherwise click on the “+ Create” button located in the upper right-hand corner of the screen. (see figure 1)

Figure 1

c) Enter a procedure name and description.

d) Click on “Create Procedure” button.

e) Under Procedure Parts, click on “+ Add item”

f) To select existing Item(s), click on the “ + “ or “ > “ for each item.

i) Click on “Create Item” button if there is no existing Item of interest.

g) For each Item in the gray lines, click on the “˅” symbol at the end of the gray line to specify the corresponding Template to use. (see firgue 2)

Figure 2

h) Click on “Update Procedure” button to save your changes. Failure to do so will result in your changes being lost. (see figure 3)

Figure 3

i) The new procedure shows up on your “Stored Procedures” list in the left menu.

i) NOTE: “Stored Procedures” is different from “Procedures” in the menu. “Procedures” (described later) is where the end user goes to start a procedure (answering questions, etc.).

j) From “Stored Procedures” you can edit each procedure by clicking on the “Edit” link to the right of each. Be sure to save the changes.

k) Once created, your “Stored Procedures” automatically populate in the “Procedures” section of the left menu for the end user to see.
