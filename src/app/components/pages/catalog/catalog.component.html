How to complete items out of the catalog

Video Guide

Walk Through

a) The Catalog section is a repository for your all your individual Items. They are visible and available to the end user to complete templates/forms. Forms can be accessed from here or from the Procedures section. (see figure 1)

Figure 1

b) Click on any Item in the Catalog to see its related details.

c) You will see areas for Updates (recently completed forms) and Sheets (recent and archived forms). (see figure 2)

Firgure 2

d) Click on “Start Form” to bring up templates/forms related to this Item.

e) Click “Start” to begin completing the Template/form. (figure 3)

i) NOTE: If no questions/steps show up, none were created in the Template/form. Select “Discard” and refer to Template Builder Section 5d above to add questions or steps to the Template/Form.

Figure 3

f) Answer the questions or follow the steps.

g) Click on “Complete” when finished.

i) In a few seconds, the completed Template/form will show up on the page.

ii) Click “View” to the right of the completed form name to view the details of the steps/responses. (see figure 4)

Figure 4
