Removal of an incorrectly initiated procedure

How to delete a submition for a procedure

Video Guide

Walk Through

a) If you mistakenly begin the incorrect Procedure (e.g., if you have more than one available to you), complete the following steps:

i) Click on “Save for Later”.

ii) This will take you back to the Procedures page and show up as an “In Progress” procedure.

iii) Click on the Procedure name to reopen it.

iv) Click on the “Garbage Can” near the top righthand part of the screen. (see figure 1)

Figure 1

v) Click on “Permanent Delete” in the pop-up screen.

b) If you mistakenly begin the incorrect Template/form (e.g., if you have more than one available to you), complete the following steps:

i) Click on the “Garbage Can” near the top righthand part of the screen.

ii) Click on “Permanent Delete” in the pop-up screen. (see figure 2)
