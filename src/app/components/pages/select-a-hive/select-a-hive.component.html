Walk Through

a) Locate the Hive you wish to work on by clicking on Manage Hives in the left-hand menu.

b) Click on the three vertical dots next to the Hive name.

c) Click on “View Hive.”

Reminder: “Edit Hive” is only for adding/editing user access.

d) When viewing the Hive, the Hive Name will appear at the top-left of the screen, directly below your name.
