Create and Manage Items

a) An Item is one level above the templates (forms) you created in Section 5. Multiple templates can be attached to an Item. Items can stand alone or roll up to a Procedure (see Section 7). All your Items are listed in the Catalog on the left menu (see Section 8).

b) Click on “Item Manager” from the left menu.

c) If this is the first new Item in a Hive, simply enter an item name (required), searchable tags, and reference number (e.g., vehicle license plate number), as needed.

i) If other Items already exist, click on “+ Create” at the top righthand part of the screen to bring up a new Item box

ii) Click “Enter” on your keyboard after creating each tag word or phrase to create multiple tags

d) Attach the relevant template(s) by clicking on the “ + “ or “ > “.

i) Remove extra templates from the item by clicking on the garbage can. These will be the questions or action steps the user follows for this Item.

e) Click on “Create Item” towards the upper right part of the active box. The information will be lost if you do not do so.

f) The Item now shows up on your Item Manager list in the left menu
