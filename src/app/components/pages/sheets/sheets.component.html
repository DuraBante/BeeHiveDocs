How to use/read the sheets section

Walk Through

a) The Sheets category in the menu is a list of all completed Templates/forms, as completed by end users. They are read-only and can be viewed by managers and end users.

b) Click on “Sheets” on the left menu.

c) This shows all completed Templates/forms from all end users. Click on any to view the details/responses. (see figure 1)
